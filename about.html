About Us

Where good food and great company come together

Our Story

Founded in 2015, KasiZist began with a simple idea
to serve authentic, flavorful dishes made from fresh,
locally sourced ingredients.
What started as a small kitchen has grown into a beloved
gathering place for family and friends who
share a love for good food and warm conversation.

Our Mission

We believe every meal tells a story. That’s why we cook with passion, serve with care,
and welcome every guest like family. Our goal is to create experiences that nourish both body and soul.

Meet The Team

Chef Thabiso

With over 15 years in the culinary industry, Chef Thabo
brings creativity and passion to every dish.
Known for blending traditional South African flavors with modern techniques,
he believes that food should tell a story — one that celebrates culture and connection.

Chef Dineo

Dineo’s love for baking began in her grandmother’s
kitchen. Today, she crafts elegant desserts
that combine comfort and artistry. From delicate pastries to decadent cakes,
her creations are a true reflection of sweetness and skill.

Chef Ndumiso

Energetic and detail-oriented, Chef Ndumiso ensures consistency and
excellence in every plate that leaves the kitchen. His teamwork,
precision, and dedication to flavor make
him an essential part of the restaurant’s heartbeat.
